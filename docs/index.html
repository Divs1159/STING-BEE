  • STCray: We introduce STCray, the first X-ray baggage security dataset with 46,642 image-caption paired scans spanning 21 categories, including Improvised Explosive Devices (IEDs) and 3D-printed firearms. The dataset follows the STING protocol for realistic simulation.

  • STING Protocol: We developed the Strategic Threat ConcealING (STING) protocol, systematically varying the position, angular placement, and occlusion of threats to enhance dataset diversity and realism.

  • STING-BEE: A domain-aware visual AI assistant trained on instruction-following datasets derived from STCray. STING-BEE unifies scene comprehension, referring threat localization, visual grounding, and VQA.
